Tribute to Steve Jobs

"The people who are crazy enough to think they can change the world are the ones who do"

TIMELINE

Feb 24, 1955 Steven Paul Jobs was born in San Francisco, California, to students Abdul Fattah Jandali and Joanne Carole Schieble who were unmarried at the time and gave him up for adoption.

1961 The Jobs family moves to Mountain View, Calif., part of what later become known as Silicon Valley.

August 9, 2011 Apple shares edge past those of Exxon Mobil to become the most valuable U.S. company, in terms of market capitalization.

August 24, 2011 Resigns from Apple CEO post; becomes chairman
